

# Complexia Landing Page — Anthropic-Style

A clean, elegant, research-focused landing page inspired by Anthropic's classic design aesthetic: lots of whitespace, muted earth tones, serif headings, and a calm, intellectual vibe.

## Design Language
- **Colors**: Warm cream/beige background (#F5F0E8-ish), dark charcoal text, subtle tan accents
- **Typography**: Serif font for headings (Georgia/Times), clean sans-serif for body
- **Layout**: Generous whitespace, centered content, minimal visual clutter
- **Animations**: Subtle fade-in on scroll

## Sections

1. **Hero** — Full-height section with the Complexia name, a concise tagline about the AI product, and a "Learn more" or "Get started" CTA button. Minimal and striking.

2. **Research** — A section highlighting Complexia's research focus with 3-4 cards/blocks describing key research areas (e.g., "Scalable AI Systems", "Interpretability", "Alignment"). Each with a short description and a subtle arrow link.

3. **Safety & Responsibility** — A dedicated section emphasizing Complexia's commitment to safe and responsible AI development. Clean text-heavy layout with a supporting statement.

4. **Company / About** — Brief section about Complexia's mission, team philosophy, and values. Simple layout with a quote or mission statement.

5. **Footer** — Minimal footer with navigation links, social links, and copyright.

## Navigation
- Sticky top navbar with logo text on the left and nav links (Research, Safety, Company) on the right. Clean, no background until scrolled.
